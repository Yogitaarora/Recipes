DESCRIPTION :

This is the best chinese  fried rice you’ll make – loaded with vegetables, spicy and super easy! Made with sichuan sauce.

INGREDIENTS :

  • 2 tablespoons Vegetable Oil
  • 1 teaspoon chopped Garlic
  • 2 tablespoons chopped Celery ((optional))
  • 1 1/2 cups finely diced Vegetables ((mushroom, beans, carrots, corn, peas etc))
  • 3 cups Rice
  • 3 tablespoons Schezwan Sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Soy Sauce
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Diced Green Onions and Sesame Seeds for topping
 

INSTRUCTIONS :

  1. Heat a wok or frying pan, and once the pan is really hot, add oil, garlic and celery to it. Cook for about 30 seconds and add the vegetables.
  2. Stir fry the vegetables on high heat for two to three minutes and add schezwan sauce, soy sauce, salt and pepper to the pan. Mix well, and add the rice.
  3. Toss the rice to coat with the sauce, and stir around for another two minutes. The rice might char at the bottom of the pan which is okay but keep the heat high. Switch off the flame, and serve hot topped with green onions and sesame seeds.
